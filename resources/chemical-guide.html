Brand: Taski

Primary Uses

  • Soap scum removal

  • Rinse completely

  • Ceramic sinks

Shelley Spray - Oven Cleaner

Brand: Shelley

Primary Uses

  • Oven interior
  • Oven racks
  • Heavy grease removal
  • Baked-on food

How to Use

  • Ensure oven is COOL
  • Spray generously inside
  • Let sit for 10-15 minutes
  • Scrub with sponge
  • Rinse thoroughly

Safety Tips

  • Use in well-ventilated area
  • Wear gloves always
  • Avoid breathing fumes
  • Rinse completely

Ajax - Floor Cleaner

Brand: Ajax

Primary Uses

  • Mopping all hard floors
  • Tiles
  • Vinyl
  • Laminate

How to Use

  • Add to bucket of water
  • Vacuum floors first
  • Mop in sections
  • Wring mop well
  • Let air dry

Best Practices

  • Use appropriate dilution
  • Change water when dirty
  • Mop towards the exit
  • Don't over-wet floors
