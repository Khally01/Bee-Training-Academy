üìã How to Use QR Codes

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Before generating QR codes, you must first deploy the training academy to a web server and have a live URL. Replace the placeholder URLs in the presets with your actual domain.

Deployment Steps:

  1. Deploy the training academy to your web hosting (see DEPLOYMENT.md)
  2. Get your live URL (e.g., https://training.qbees.com.au)
  3. Update the preset URLs in this file (line ~300) with your actual domain
  4. Generate QR codes for each section
  5. Download and print deployment materials

Best Practices:

  1. Test QR codes: Always scan with a phone before printing
  2. Size matters: Print QR codes at least 2cm x 2cm (larger is better)
  3. High contrast: Black QR on white background works best
  4. Include text: Add a description below the QR code
  5. Placement: Put QR codes in common areas (break room, base, vehicles)
