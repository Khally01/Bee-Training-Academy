üß∫ Rejected Linen Procedures

What are Rejected Linens? Linens we receive from our supplier that have stains, rips, or are in bad condition and cannot be used for cleans.

Why This Matters

When you properly identify and separate rejected linens, we can:

  • Return them to our linen supplier
  • Get our money back for unusable linens
  • Avoid paying for linens we couldn't use

What to Do:

‚ùå Don't Do This:

  • Don't put rejected linens in the dirties trolley - this means we used them and must pay
  • Don't use damaged linens for jobs
  • Don't throw them away

üí° Pro Tips for Efficiency

Work Smarter, Not Harder

  • TIP Spray chemicals and let them sit while you work on other areas
  • TIP Group similar tasks together (all dusting, then all wiping, then all vacuuming)
  • TIP Start from the farthest room and work toward the exit
  • TIP Keep your cleaning kit organized - time wasted looking for supplies adds up!
  • TIP Take photos as you go, not all at the end
  • TIP Check vacuum battery at arrival, plug in if needed
  • TIP Prepare tomorrow's linens at base today to save morning time
